## Behavioral Interviews

- Bezos -> Amazon -> Tech industry

STAR Method

Situation
Task
Action
Result

"Tell me about a time when..."

### Goal as an Interviewer
    - Signal to Noise
    - Will this person be good at the job

### Goal as an Interviewee - Can I show a pattern of delivering measurable results

### Situation
 - We had a bug in production that cost us $1000/day

### Task
 - I was tasked with resolving the bug and reducing our cost to at most $500/day

### Action
 - I asked contacts in the industry how to debug this issue given with how new our company was
 with the technology at the time and due to the quick turnaround needed

### Result
 -  Using the tools and techniques I was recommended and some trial and error I was able to lower costs to just
 $100/day in less than 1 week, saving the company $1000000 over the next year.

    - Highlight 5-10 wins from your career
        - 5 very different wins 
         - leading teams or helping lead out an initiative
         - technical accomplishment (project - hardest thing that you solved for your Major Projects)
         - disagreements
    - Go into chatGPT to help tease out those wins
    - The STAR story from that win
    - reverse engineer the question that story would answer
