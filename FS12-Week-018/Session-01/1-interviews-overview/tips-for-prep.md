## Prep for Technical Interviews

1. 30-40 challenges on codewars.com
2. pramp.com -> prep for actual interviews - 5-10 of these
3. should be pretty well prepped for 1st real interviews

## Mock Interviews

- Do with a peer (pramp.com)
- Coach on Codecademy -> 1 on 1 prep
- Safe setting where you can practice before you "play"
- You should be able to get hints and not be stuck
