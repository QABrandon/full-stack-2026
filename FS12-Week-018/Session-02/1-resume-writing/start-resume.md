1. Visit **ohmycv.app**
2. Start a new resume
3. Paste in **alice-example-resume.md**
4. Use ChatGPT, Claude.ai, Gemini, etc., to update the template with your own resume content:

   1. Add your personal details in the top section
   2. Change the summary according to the role you want and your skills
   3. Update the skills section to include additional skills/tools:

      - Photoshop / Figma / design experience
      - Other coding languages
      - Linux skills

   4. Update the projects section:

      - Add links / QR codes to videos
      - Use bit.ly for QR codes and URL shortening

   5. Education section
   6. Professional experience:

      - Accomplished X, as measured by Y, doing Z (avoid repeating that exact phrase pattern)

   7. Volunteer experience:

      - Hobbies
      - Notable achievements
5. Once done export as PDF
