# Job Search Resources & Strategy

## Key Concepts

### Job Application Strategy

- **Apply quickly:** Submit applications within 24 hours of the job posting (or less)
- **Time budget:** Spend at most 20% of your total job-search time on applications themselves — reserve the rest for networking, skill-building, and interview prep
