## There are several ways that you could prepare to present

1. Live Presentation
- This is the most ideal as it helps you to practice on the job skills in a safe environment
a. You demo with a live site or just a mock up, giving the pitch, where your project is, and the long term vision
b. You demo with a slide show, take screen caps at specific times to show functionality
2. Recorded Presentation
 - You may not be able to attend the final day or you might not feel able to present for whatever reason, if so I would love a recorded version
 a. You can use the same live demo approach or a slide show
  - Recording On Mac might be easiest with the screen recorder feature built into the quicktime app, or another app like loom may help
  - Recording on windows might be easiest with the Snippets app on windows 11
