About Me

Built for thoughtful design, strong systems, and clean execution.

I’m Brandon Mosco, founder of BM Web Studio and a Webflow-focused designer, developer, and technical problem solver. I help businesses modernize outdated websites, improve user experience, and create digital systems that are easier to manage, scale, and maintain over time.

My background spans more than 13 years in web development, with the last 7+ years focused heavily on Webflow. Along the way, I’ve also built deep experience in QA, accessibility, responsive testing, CMS structure, and front-end implementation. That mix lets me bridge the gap between design, development, and long-term site quality.

I’m most energized by projects that go beyond the basic brochure site: migrations, custom CMS builds, interactive components, advanced layouts, automation, and technical UX challenges that need real thinking behind them. I care about building work that looks polished, performs well, and actually supports the goals of the business.
